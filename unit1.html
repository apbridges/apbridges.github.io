My Favorite Video Games

I enjoy playing videos games, whether it be with friends and family, or alone. These are my top three favorite games.

  1. Halo Reach
    • I played this game alot with my friends.
    • This was the only game I played for a whole year.
    • I had a lot of fun making maps in Forge.


  2. Dark Souls
    • I really enjoyed it's aesthetic and that it wasn't a generic fantasy game.
    • It was a challenging game that, even though infuriating at times, was replayable.
    • It's version of storytelling I thought was really well done.


  3. Minecraft
    • This is the game I probably played most with my Dad.
    • Similarly with Halo Reach, this was the only game I played for a long time.
    • I always found that the game was really relaxing and calming whenever I played it.
