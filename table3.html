BBQ Hamburger
Ingredient Name Amount Picture of Ingredient
Hamburger Meat 8 Oz.
Hamburger Buns 1 pair of buns
BBQ Sauce 2 Tblsp
Worcestershire Sauce 1 Tblsp
Condiments However much you want
Directions:
1. Get all ingredients as well as a pan out.
2. Mix 8 Oz. of meat with 2 tblsp of BBQ sauce and 1 tblsp of worcestershire sauce.
3. Cook the meat for ~10 min. on medium heat.
4. (Optional) Cook bottems of buns for ~1 min. to add flavor.
5. Put hamburger patty between buns and add any wanted condiments.
Finished BBQ Burger
